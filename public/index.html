About me

Who is Trier Buhrsmith?

I grew up in Minneapolis, MN and am currently based here. I attended DePaul University from 2014-2018 and graduated with a BS in International Studies. In 2019, after serving and bartending full-time, I embarked on an international adventure to Prague, Czechia, where I studied the English language and recieved a certificate in Teaching English as a Foreign Language (TEFL). I returned home, and was faced with the challenge of COVID-19 and the question "what now?" While furloughed, I decided that a great use of time would be learning a new, versatile, and interesting skill. Remembering that I had taken an Intro to Web Design course in my undergrad, and loved it, I decided to enroll in an intensive three month Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Minnesota. After working tirelessly for three months straight, I graduated with an entirely new repetoire of skills!

I am now seeking employment in the tech world! While I am still learning, I have a full-stack, a MERN stack to be specific, of knowledge about developing and designing websites.
